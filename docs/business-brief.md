# TENG Smart Parking Business Brief

Date: 2026-07-11
Company: TENG Asset Co., Ltd.

## Core Idea

TENG Smart Parking helps owners of vacant land in central Ubon Ratchathani convert idle land into automated parking revenue. Customers do not need to download an app, take a paper ticket, or interact with staff. They scan a QR code, pay with PromptPay QR, and the gate opens automatically.

## Target Partner

The first business customer is a vacant land owner who wants to reduce the burden of unused land and generate revenue without running parking operations themselves.

## Pilot Scope

- 10-20 parking bays
- One controlled entry and one controlled exit, or one shared gate for very small sites
- QR-first customer flow
- ESP32 gate controller
- Payment-backed session ticket
- Basic dashboard and exported revenue report

## Partner Value Proposition

- Turn idle land into recurring income
- Avoid hiring full-time parking attendants
- Start with lower capital expenditure than a full staffed parking lot
- Receive transparent revenue reporting
- Use TENG as the operator and technology provider

## Customer Value Proposition

- No app install
- No paper slip
- Fast QR payment
- Clear package choices
- Easy exit scan and overtime payment

## Recommended Revenue Model

Use revenue sharing for early pilots:

- Gross parking revenue is collected through TENG payment rails.
- Payment gateway fees and direct operating costs are deducted first.
- Net operating revenue is split between land owner and TENG.

Initial negotiation anchor:

- Land owner: 50-60% of net operating revenue
- TENG: 40-50% of net operating revenue

Alternative for high-demand land:

- Minimum monthly guarantee plus revenue share above the guarantee.

## Pilot Success Criteria

- The system operates without a full-time attendant.
- More than 95% of entries and exits complete without manual intervention.
- Daily revenue reports can be shown to the land owner.
- Hardware survives normal outdoor operation.
- Customer support issues are simple enough for TENG to handle remotely.
